About LANBox

Inspired by the PirateBox Project. LANBox is a offline, self-contained mobile collaboration and file sharing device. LANBox creates a local wireless network where users can chat, share images, files and other digital media. LANBox can also be used for distributing information in emergency situations.

LANBox is designed in a way to not require an active internet connecion to work. Thus the data will not leave the device and preserve the privacy of the users.

LANBox is developed by MrMinemeet. Contribution and feedback is welcomed.
